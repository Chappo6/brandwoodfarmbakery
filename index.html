About Us

Brandwood Farm Bakery is a family-run bakery focused on traditional techniques and seasonal ingredients. We hand-shape dough, proof slowly, and bake in small batches so every loaf has character.

We deliver locally and offer weekly subscriptions for staples like sourdough, croissants, and cookies. Want to learn more or visit the bakehouse? Email us or follow along on Instagram.
